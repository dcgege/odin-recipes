Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork ribs or pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 cup tamarind paste or 1 packet tamarind soup base mix
  • 2 cups daikon radish, sliced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup okra, trimmed
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong) or spinach
  • 2-3 green chili peppers (optional)
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork, onion, and tomatoes. Reduce heat and simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  2. Add tamarind paste or tamarind soup base mix to the pot. Stir well to dissolve.
  3. Add daikon radish and cook for about 10 minutes.
  4. Add green beans, eggplant, and okra. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  5. Add water spinach (kangkong) and green chili peppers (if using). Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the greens are wilted.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the sourness by adding more tamarind paste if needed.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
