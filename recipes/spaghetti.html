Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)
    Steps
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss to combine.
  5. Serve with fresh basil if desired.
